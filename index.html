My-Story

by Joselyn Melendez

My Daily routine:

I wake up and brush my teeth.

Then I get dressed and go to school.

It takes about 30 minutes to get to Lane if traffic isnt bad.

At school I go to all of my classes.

I take Driver's Ed, Chemistry, Trig., AP World History, Spanish, Programming, and English II.

I am in Omega which is the humanities version of Alpha.

Basically that means my english and history classes are at a higher level than core english classes.

(Honestly I didn't really like my english teacher, but its the end of the year so I'll be okay.)

After school I usually have dance practice from 3:30 to 6:30.

After practice I go home and take a shower.

Then I eat dinner and do my homework.

Then I mess around on my phone for way longer than I'd like to admit

Then I fall alseep.
